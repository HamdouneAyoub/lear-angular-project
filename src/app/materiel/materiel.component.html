Services

Our services

  • Automotive Seating

    Lear provides a range of automotive seating systems, including complete seat assemblies, seat structures, seat trim covers,and headrests. Lear's seating systems are designed to provide comfort, safety, and convenience to the driver and passengers.

  • Automotive electronics

    Lear Corporation also provides automotive electronics solutions, including connectivity systems, audio systems, navigation systems, and vehicle information and entertainment systems , improving vehicle safety, and enabling the development of more advanced and intelligent vehicles

  • Thermal management

    Lear Corporation provides thermal management solutions for vehicles, including heated and ventilated seats, heating and air conditioning systems, and cooling systems for electric vehicle batteries , Lear's thermal management solutions are focused on maximizing the performance

  • Manufacturing

    Lear Corporation offers manufacturing services to its customers, including automotive parts manufacturing, component assembly and production of complete seating systems

  • Automotive interiors

    Lear Corporation also offers high-quality interiors for vehicles, including dashboards, door panels, virtual prototyping and modeling , center consoles and other interior components.

  • Research and development

    Lear Corporation invests in the research and development of innovative technologies to improve the performance, safety and comfort of automotive seating and electronic systems
